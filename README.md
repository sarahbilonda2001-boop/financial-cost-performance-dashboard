📊 Financial Cost & Sales Performance Analytics Dashboard
📌 Project Overview

This Power BI project delivers an end-to-end analysis of financial performance and sales performance across the years 2023 and 2024.

The dashboard helps business stakeholders monitor cost efficiency, revenue growth, and profitability trends to support data-driven decision making.

It is structured into two main analytical views:

Financial Cost Performance Analysis
Sales Performance Analysis
🎯 Business Objective

To provide a clear and interactive view of:

Cost optimization and control
Revenue and profit performance tracking
Year-over-year (YoY) business comparison (2023 vs 2024)
Identification of high-performing and low-performing segments

📄 Dashboard Structure
💰 1. Financial Cost Performance Dashboard

This section focuses on analyzing cost distribution and financial efficiency.

📊 Key Features:
Monthly Cost Analysis (2023 vs 2024)
Cost Breakdown by Country
Total Cost Tracking
Price and Sales Monitoring
🔍 Key Insights:
Identification of cost variations over time
Detection of high-cost countries impacting profitability
Year-over-year financial performance comparison

📊 2. Sales Performance Dashboard

This section focuses on revenue generation and profitability analysis.

📊 Key Features:

Revenue Trend Analysis (Current Month vs Previous Month)
Revenue by Channel (Retail / Wholesale)
Gross Profit by Region
Gross Profit by Customer Segment
KPI Cards (Total Sales, Revenue, Gross Profit)
YoY % Increase / Decrease (2023 vs 2024)

🔍 Key Insights:

Revenue growth trends across time
Profitability distribution by region and segment
Channel performance comparison
Business growth and decline tracking

📌 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
💰 Total Revenue
📈 Total Sales
💵 Gross Profit
📊 Cost Efficiency Metrics
📉 YoY Growth % (Revenue, Sales, Profit)
🛠 Tools & Technologies
Power BI
Power Query (Data Cleaning & Transformation)
DAX (Data Modeling & Calculations)
Data Visualization Techniques
📈 Business Impact

This dashboard enables:

Faster financial decision-making
Identification of cost reduction opportunities
Monitoring of sales performance in real time
Better understanding of customer segment profitability
